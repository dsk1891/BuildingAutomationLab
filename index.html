Building Automation Laboratory

A Building Automation Laboratory is an academic or commercial facility designed for researching, teaching, and testing Building Automation and Control Systems (BACS). These labs focus on the integration of HVAC,lighting, security, and fire safety systems to optimize energy efficiency, safety, and occupant comfort.
